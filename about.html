About Superfan Studios

Superfan Studios is a creative-first digital agency that builds experiences and stories for brands, creators, and communities. We specialize in designing engaging platforms and fan-driven experiences that bridge the gap between entertainment and technology.

Our work spans across multiple genres — from English Horror and Hindi Horror to Mythology, Music, Comedy, and Tech.

With every project, we aim to make fandoms stronger by giving fans immersive ways to connect with the content they love. At Superfan Studios, we believe the future of digital storytelling is interactive, bold, and fan-centric.
